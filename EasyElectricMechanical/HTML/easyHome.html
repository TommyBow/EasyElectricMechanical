About Us

Easy Electric Mechanical (Pty) Ltd is a level 2 Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment company based in eMalahleni. We strive to deliver the best service by consistently meeting and exceeding our customers' expectations. Our vision is to continue to expand to enable us to meet the demands of the customer.

What We Do

Tailored to meet your diverse needs and specifications, our comprehensive range of services is executed by a team with extensive knowledge and expertise. We take pride in delivering top-notch workmanship, ensuring excellence in all facets of steelwork. Committed to providing high-quality services at prices aligned with market standards, we stand out as a reliable choice for your steelwork needs.

Machining

Crafting precision machined components is our specialty, encompassing a spectrum of services such as bore and key manufacturing, milling, slotting, and threading.

Fabrication

Our expertise extends to steel fabrication and installation, covering a diverse range of services. This includes the creation and installation of furnace water-cooled components, both new installations and refurbishments. Our proficiency extends to structural repairs, ensuring the longevity and reliability of your steel structures.

Thermal Insulation

Our scope encompasses the provision of electrical and thermal furnace insulation components, featuring an array of materials such as Mica phlogophite, GP03, SG200, NAD500, G7 silicon glass, G10, and G11 epoxy.
