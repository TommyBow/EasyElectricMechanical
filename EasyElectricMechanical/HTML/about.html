About us

Founded in 1995, Easy Electric Mechanical has rapidly grown into a reputable leader in the electrical and mechanical industry. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the market. As a level 2 Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment company headquartered in eMalahleni, we take pride in contributing to the economic empowerment and development of the local community. At Easy Electric Mechanical, we specialize in providing a comprehensive range of electrical and mechanical solutions, catering to the diverse needs of our clients. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering high-quality services that adhere to industry standards and regulations. In addition to our commitment to service excellence, we prioritize sustainability and environmental responsibility in our operations. Easy Electric Mechanical is dedicated to implementing eco-friendly practices and technologies, ensuring that our impact on the environment is minimized. As we continue to grow, our vision remains focused on meeting and exceeding customer expectations. We believe in fostering long-term partnerships with our clients, built on trust, reliability, and the consistent delivery of top-notch services. Our ongoing expansion efforts are geared towards better serving our clients and staying ahead of industry trends and innovations.
